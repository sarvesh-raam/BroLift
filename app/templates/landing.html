How BroLift Works

Three easy steps to your next shared ride

01

Register with SRM Email

Sign up using your SRM IST email (e.g. st6546@srmist.edu.in). Only verified SRM students can access BroLift.

02

Host or Find a Ride

Post your ride with start location, time, and seats — or search for rides by date near your pickup point.

03

Split Fuel and Go

Fuel cost is automatically split equally among all riders. Everyone saves money. Everyone wins.
